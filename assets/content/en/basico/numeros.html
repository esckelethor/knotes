Centenas
100 -> a/one hundred
200 -> two hundred
300 -> three hundred

Para unir centenas y decenas se utiliza and
255 -> two hundred and fifty-five
648 -> six hundred and forty-eight

Millares
1000 -> a/one thousand
2000 -> two thousand
10000 -> ten thousand

Millones
1000000 -> a/one million
2000000 -> two million
6000000 -> six million

Ej: 6279 -> six thousand two hundred and seventy-nine
